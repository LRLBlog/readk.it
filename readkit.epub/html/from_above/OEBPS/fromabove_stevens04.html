Albert Stevens, United States 1886–1949. Bomber Formation over San Francisco Bay, California, 1920s. Gelatin silver print. Gift of Ansel and Virginia Adams 78.100.38. Courtesy of the U.S. Air Service and the Center for Creative Photography

The last vestiges of World War I aircraft technology. These Curtiss B-2 Condors were dreadfully obsolete by 1930 when this photo was taken. It would be another seven years before the Army Air Service acquired a truly modern bomber. —James Stemm, Curator of Collections, Pima Air & Space Museum
