This image of a quarry has a wealth of different textures—from the smooth surface that appears to be in active use for mining, to the finely-ridged eroded hill in the upper left, to several different patterns of vegetation. Textures provide valuable clues in understanding what we’re seeing in an aerial photograph. —Barbara Trapido-Lurie, Cartographer
