COMMENTATOR

Barbara Trapido-Lurie

Barbara Trapido-Lurie is a research professional in the School of Geographical Sciences and Urban Planning at Arizona State University. In this role, she supports efforts of the geography and urban planning faculty by creating maps, managing the school’s web site, and coordinating internships. She also teaches courses in Geographic Information Technologies and Cartography and Geographic Visualization. Larger cartographic projects include 76 maps & other illustrations for Metropolitan Phoenix by Patricia Gober, and numerous maps for the Arizona Geographic Alliance. Trapido-Lurie earned an MA in geography from the University of Hawaii and a B.A. in art from Pomona College.
