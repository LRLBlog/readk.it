COMMENTATOR

Bo Svoma

Bo Svoma is a climatologist and physical geographer working as a Research Associate and Lecturer at the School of Geographical Sciences and Urban Planning at Arizona State University. Born in Flagstaff and raised in Tempe, Arizona, he received all his degrees from ASU. He has Bachelor of Science degrees in Mathematics and Geography and received his PhD in Geography in 2011.
