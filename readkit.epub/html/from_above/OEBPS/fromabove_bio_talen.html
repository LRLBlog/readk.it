COMMENTATOR

Emily Talen

Emily Talen, Phd, FAICP, is a Professor at Arizona State University in the School of Geographical Sciences and Urban Planning, and a senior sustainability scientist in ASU’s School of Sustainability. She holds a Ph.D in urban geography from the University of California, Santa Barbara. Her most recent book is City Rules (Island Press, 2012), which explores the coded dimension of urban form.
