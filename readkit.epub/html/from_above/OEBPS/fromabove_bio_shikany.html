COMMENTATOR

Peter Shikany

Peter Shikany founded P.S. Studios in 1989. Since then he has led this Phoenix based firm to produce award-winning design for national, regional, and local clients. Working in all mediums P.S. Studios has provided design for brands such as illy espresso, Desert Botanical Garden, The Design School at ASU, Ballet Arizona, and University of Phoenix. They have also produced many award-winning books and catalogs, recently winning top design awards for three exhibit catalogs for Phoenix Art Museum.
