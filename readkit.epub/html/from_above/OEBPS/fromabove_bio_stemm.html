COMMENTATOR

James Stemm

James is a native of Albuquerque, New Mexico and has been an aviation fanatic since his first time on an airliner at the age of 3 and an avid student of history since high school. He received a Bachelor’s degree in History from the University of New Mexico in 1990 and a Master’s degree in Public History from New Mexico State University in 1994. He was hired as the Registrar for the Pima Air & Space Museum in 1996. James received a promotion to Assistant Curator in 2002. He has held the position of Curator of Collections at the Pima Air & Space Museum since 2006.
