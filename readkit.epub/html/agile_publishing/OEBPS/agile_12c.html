Phoenix Art Museum
Results, so far …

Less than 30 hours spent
creating the e-book and download page.

First 11 days: 105 unique pageviews, 37 downloads and 6 email deliveries of the e-book. On track for 500 downloads by exhibition end.

Early thoughts and lessons:

Have not yet promoted the e-book outside the exhibition itself.

Technical hurdles with in-gallery downloads: Large file size, browser/QR reader support of .epub file type.

Project team was only curator and e-book publisher, but should have also included marketing.
