Agile tools are …

Universal Doc Formats

Content at its most basic. The more open and widely used a format is, the less expensive and easier to create and read it will be: PDF · HTML

Free E-Book Tools

There are an increasing number of free, often open source e-book creation tools that use the .epub file format, the most open and widely used e-book format available, and itself built on HTML: OSCI Toolkit · PressBooks · Smashwords · The People’s E-Book

Low-cost App Tools

There are a number of popular tools for low cost, low barrier-to-entry app and app-like publishing. Though these tend to focus solely on the iPad, and as such are arguably less agile: iBooks Author · Adobe DPS · Aerbook · Inkling Habitat

Print on Demand

Agile publishing is not limited to digital alone. Print on demand services can now handle anything from simple black and white paperbacks to full-color magazines and catalogues: Lulu · Blurb · MagCloud
